Who Are We

Codeyoung is a platform where your kids can let their imaginations run wild. Students are taught to be creative, innovative and use coding in the most unusual way possible.At Codeyoung, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds in the world of coding and Stem education.

What is Codeyoung Coding Course

Codeyoung is a company that operates a coding platform dedicated to developing cognitive skills and creative thinking among children. The company offers various coding courses including an introduction to programming, web development, Python language, and introduction to machine learning, thereby enabling kids to Improve problem-solving skills.

When where we founded

Codeyoung was founded in the year 2018.It was Founded by IIT-Delhi alumni Shailendra Dhakad and Rupika Taneja in November 2019, Codeyoung is on a mission to develop cognitive skills and creative thinking among children through coding.

Our Programmes

Codeyoung offers four programmes: Scratch, app development, web development,and Python for the age groups of 5-9, 9-14, and 14-16.Each course includes 48 classes, and costs Rs 19,999. How do we Teach

Codeyoung’s course content is complementary to the school curriculum, and the trainers work towards improving a child’s cognitive abilities, creative, and logical thinking. At the end of the coding programme, K-12 students can build simple games, animations, and websites using Scratch.
